Perl 6 Tablets

These are the Perl 6 Tablets, a comprehensive manual, aimed to support many different ways of learning. The content is nicely sorted and indexed and many links allow you to follow your interest freely. For a lightweight introduction, try my Perl 6 Tutorial or the Book Using Perl 6 or Learning Perl 6.
If that is too easy, read the specs.
